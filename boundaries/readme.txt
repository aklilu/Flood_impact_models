Uganda catchments
Shapefile of the upstream basins for three districts: Moyo, Soroti, Sironko.
the "overlap (%)" field in the attribute table stands for the percentage of the area of every basin (level 6) which contributes to feed each district of interest. Measure of the upstream area for every district.
Watersheds from Hydrosheds  (https://hydrosheds.org/), level of detail 06.
